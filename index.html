Draw to Save - Save the Dog

Draw your way to save the dog from peril!

About the Game

Draw to Save - Save the Dog is a unique puzzle game where players use their drawing skills to rescue a cute dog from tricky situations. Each level presents different challenges, from dangerous obstacles to tricky traps. Draw creative and effective lines to protect the dog and ensure its safety. As you progress, the levels get more difficult, and the dog faces even bigger threats!

HOW TO PLAY

  • Draw Lines

    Use your finger or mouse to draw protective lines and save the dog from hazards like falling objects, sharp spikes, and more.

  • Complete Challenges

    Each level has a unique challenge where you need to think creatively to draw the best protective line.

  • Unlock New Levels

    Progress through hundreds of levels with increasing difficulty and diverse obstacles.

  • Rescue the Dog

    Each level ends with the dog being saved. The better your drawing, the safer your dog will be!

  • Collect Stars

    Earn stars for completing levels and use them to unlock fun outfits and accessories for your dog.
